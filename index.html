About Hamilton Decorating

Craftsmen who care about the finish — and the family inside

Based in Hastings, East Sussex, Hamilton Decorating brings years of hands-on experience to every home and business we work in. We treat every room as if it were our own — careful prep, quality materials, and a clean site from start to finish.

From single rooms to full property renovations, we work across East Sussex and Tunbridge Wells. No shortcuts. No mess left behind. Just a result you'll love.

Why Hamilton Decorating

The difference is in the detail

I

Meticulous prep work

Great results start before a brush is lifted. We fill, sand, and prime every surface properly — no shortcuts, no peeling down the line.

II

Premium materials only

We specify the right product for every surface — Farrow & Ball, Dulux Trade, Little Greene. Materials that hold their colour year after year.

III

Respectful of your home

Full dust sheets, careful masking, and a tidy site every day. We leave your home clean — not just our work, but every area we move through.
